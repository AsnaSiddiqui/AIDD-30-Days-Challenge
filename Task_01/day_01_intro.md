## My Reflection on AI-Driven Development (AIDD)

### What does AI-Driven Development Means to Me?

AI-Driven Development ne mujhe coding ko ek nayi nazar se dekhna sikhaya. Pehle jo cheeze mushkil lagti thi, AI unhe simple steps me explain kar deta hai.
Jab koi cheez samajh nahi aati, AI mujhe step-by-step samjha deta hai aur main easy feel karti hoon.
AI se coding fast bhi hoti hai aur mushkil cheeze bhi samajh aa jati hain.
Main AI ko ek helper ya partner ki tarah dekhti hoon, jo mera kaam smooth kar deta hai.
Mere liye AI ek co-developer jaisa ban gaya hai.

###  How do I see the future of Human–AI Collaboration?

Mujhe lagta hai aane wale time me developers aur AI ek strong team ki tarah kaam karenge. AI saara repetitive kaam le lega aur human decision-making karega. Isi collaboration ki wajah se development zyada creative aur fast ho jayegi.
To mere liye Human–AI Collaboration ka future equal partnership jaisa ho ga.
